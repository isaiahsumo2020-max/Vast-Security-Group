WHO WE ARE

Professional Security Built on Integrity

VAST Security Group (Vigilance Asset Security Team) is a professional security services company dedicated to protecting lives, properties, assets, businesses, institutions, and communities. Through highly trained personnel, operational discipline, and a commitment to excellence, we provide dependable security solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

We believe security is built on trust, preparedness, accountability, and continuous improvement. Our team strives to create safe environments that allow our clients to focus on what matters most.

Our Mission

To provide professional, reliable, and innovative security solutions that protect lives, assets, businesses, and communities through vigilance, integrity, and operational excellence.

Our Vision

To become Liberia's most trusted and respected security service provider, recognized for professionalism, excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to safety.
